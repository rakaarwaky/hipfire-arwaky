## Mechanics

- 0.8B has 24 layers; 9B has 32 layers. Each layer has wo + w_down residual GEMVs (2 fusion sites/layer/token).
- 0.8B saves 48 add_inplace_f32 launches/token; 9B saves 64.
- Decode is launch-latency-bound on gfx1100 (per feedback memory), so the saving is consistent with measured tok/s.
- Win is smaller on 9B because per-token GEMV cost dominates the savings (more weights per layer).
