## Mechanics

Replaces the four-step FFN epilogue
  silu_mul_f32(gate, up, ffn_hidden)        # 1 launch
  rotate_x_mq(ffn_hidden, x_rot)            # 1 launch
  gemv_hfq3g256(w_down, x_rot, scratch)     # 1 launch
  add_inplace_f32(x, scratch)               # 1 launch
with the two-step
  fused_silu_mul_rotate_mq(gate, up, x_rot) # 1 launch
  gemv_hfq3g256_residual(w_down, x_rot, x)  # 1 launch
Two fewer kernel launches per FFN block per layer per token (32 layers
on 9B = 64 launches/token saved on top of the residual-fusion alone).
