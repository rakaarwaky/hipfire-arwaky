## Mechanics

Eliminates per-call alloc + gemv + add_inplace + free chain
(four operations, one launch + alloc + free + launch) in favour of
two launches: rotate (already required) + fused gemv_residual.
Per FFN block per layer per token (32 layers \* 2 sites = 64/token).
